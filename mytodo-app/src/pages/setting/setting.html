Settings

Back Up Data

Back up your tasks to Google Drive. You can restore them when you reinstall ToDo app. Tasks you back up are not protected by ToDo app end-to-end encryption while in Google Drive.

Retrieve Data

Before retrieving your tasks from the Google Drive you have to upload your tasks to Google Drive. Once you uploaded the tasks after that you can retrieve tasks from the Google Drive. Tasks you retrieve are not protected by ToDo app end-to-end encryption while in Google Drive.
